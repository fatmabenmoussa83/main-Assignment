Think and Grow Rich



Think and Grow Rich is a book written by Napoleon Hill released in 1937
and promoted as a personal development and self-improvement book.
He claimed to be inspired by a suggestion from business magnate and
later-philanthropist Andrew Carnegie. However there is no evidence that
the two ever met.The book is considered a classic in the personal
development genre and has been widely influential in shaping the way
people think about success and wealth.
